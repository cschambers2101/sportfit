Emergency Care

Sportfit offer emergency residential short break care for young people experiencing crisis, family relationship break downs and care and abuse issues. We use appropriate and comfortable accommodation situated on the Isle of Wight, The New Forest, Portland and Southampton. Staff are very experienced in child care and are fully trained in de escalating difficult situations. Our Emergency Care Packages are person centered and designed around the needs of the child and involve the following:

  • Work Experience (Paid and Unpaid)
  • Independent living skills
  • Education by way of private tutoring
  • Sports and fitness based activities
  • Outward bounds experiences
  • Life skills/problem solving skills

Short Breaks

Sportfit Shortbreak Care work with groups with a wide variety of special educational needs, such as autism, emotional/behavioral difficulties, ADHD, specific specialised needs and disabilities, and other all encompassing learning needs. Service users are able to make new friends and take part in a huge variety of actvities, whilst developing social skills and independent living skills in a secure and positive environment. We ensure that participation in activities is enjoyable in the hope that service users will continue to be active and eat healthily throughout their lives. Our short breaks takes place throughout the Country during school holiday times. Please click on the latest breaks tab to see when our next breaks are taking place.

Activity Support

Our activity provision is again tailor made to each service user we work with. Success based activities play an important part in building self esteem in young people and we are able to find a range of activities that suit.

Sportfit staff are adept at working with a wide range of needs and circumstances. They are able to enthuse even the most challenging of cases and find a positive way forward.

Active enjoyment, good role models, and effective implementation of activity and care plans are at the core of our activity based provision.

Domicliary Care

Sportfit support many service users needing personal care, general day to day support, meal preparations and much more within their own home environment. Our domicillary services are delivered professionally and uniquely. All service users have specific tailor made support plans and are visited by Sportfit prior to support commencing in order for staff to introduce themselves to the family/service user and for an assessment of their needs to be completed. All staff are fully experienced in care and hold current enhanced DBS checks.
